About

The 13th International Conference on Spontaneous Coherence in Excitonic Systems (ICSCE-13) is organized by CNR-NANOTEC in Lecce, Italy, and will take place in Canary Island. This event continues the well-established tradition of gathering researchers focused on quantum collective phenomena in various electronic excitations in solids, along with related effects in other physical systems.

With an emphasis on the broad concept of spontaneous coherence, ICSCE-13 will feature a diverse range of topics, including:

  • Exciton-polariton condensates, lasers, and lattices
  • Computation using exciton-polaritons
  • Exciton-polaritons in emerging material systems (TMDCs, Perovskites, etc.)
  • Organic exciton-polaritons
  • Exciton condensation
  • Cold atom lattices and alternative platforms for quantum simulation

We look forward to welcoming you to Canary Island for an engaging and insightful conference.
